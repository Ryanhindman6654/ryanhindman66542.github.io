About

Ryan is a freelance programmer. He helps make Discord bots, Games, and Websites. He has taken many programming classes and he self taught himself and many others. Up above are some pictures of his work.

Many of his programming skills are around JavaScript, Python, CSS and HTML, and Lua.

Prices:

Ryan's Programming most does work for free depending on the size. If it just a small issue it will not cost anything. To code a large project or to start will the cost will depend on:
1. What the project is
2. Size of project
3. Time needed to finish

All payments must be paid before you get access your products
Note: We do not host websites or discord bots. You are in charge of doing that .We will recommend hosting sites.
